What is Gappsy?

Gappsy is a comprehensive marketing agency directory platform that helps businesses find and connect with top marketing agencies across the United States. Our platform features:

  • Curated Top 25 rankings for every U.S. state (50 states + DC + Puerto Rico = 52 total regions)
  • Detailed agency profiles with specialties, services, and verified performance metrics
  • Direct contact and availability request system
  • Agency reviews and ratings from verified clients
  • Featured placements and spotlight sections for premium visibility

How Agencies Get Listed

Submission Process

  1. Submit Application: Fill out agency information and provide verification details
  2. Review: Our team reviews submissions based on:
    • Verified business credentials
    • Client testimonials and reviews
    • Portfolio and case studies
    • Industry reputation and awards
    • Team expertise and certifications
  3. Approval & Placement: Approved agencies receive free basic listing
  4. Optional Upgrade: Agencies can upgrade to premium placements for enhanced visibility

Top 25 Ranking Criteria

Agencies in Top 25 rankings are evaluated based on:

  • Client satisfaction scores and verified reviews
  • Project success rates and ROI delivered
  • Industry awards and recognition
  • Team expertise and certifications
  • Years in business and portfolio quality
  • Innovation and technology adoption

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gappsy free for businesses?

Yes, Gappsy is completely free for businesses searching for marketing agencies. Browse rankings, view profiles, and request availability at no cost.

How often are rankings updated?

Rankings are reviewed and updated monthly based on new data, reviews, and performance metrics.

Can agencies serve multiple states?

Yes, agencies can be listed in multiple states. Multi-state packages are available for agencies with regional or national reach.

What types of marketing agencies are listed?

Gappsy features full-service agencies as well as specialists in: digital marketing, SEO, PPC, social media, content marketing, branding, web design, email marketing, and more.

How do I verify an agency's credentials?

Each agency profile includes verification badges, client reviews, portfolio samples, and team credentials. We encourage businesses to contact agencies directly for additional verification.

Can I list an international agency?

Currently, Gappsy focuses on agencies serving U.S. markets. Agencies can be headquartered internationally but must serve U.S. clients.
