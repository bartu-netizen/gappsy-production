   Why:    Removes ambiguity. Admin knows nothing gets sent.
   Helper: "Shows which clean/eligible contacts would be sent. No contacts

   After:  "Send to Smartlead"
   Why:    Makes action obvious - sending to Smartlead, not just syncing data.
   Helper: "Sends eligible contacts to the selected Smartlead campaign."

   Before: (no note)
   After:  "Required to send contacts to Smartlead."
   Why:    Admin understands why Campaign ID is needed.
