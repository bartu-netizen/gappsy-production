KEY IMPROVEMENTS
────────────────

1. ✅ Removed Agency Info Card
   - No longer shows: [📍] Agency Name | State · Free listing
   - Cleaner, less redundant

2. ✅ Removed Duplicate Loss Statement
   - No standalone "X clients went to competitors" line
   - Only in KPI card

3. ✅ Stronger Headline
   - Mobile: 14px → Desktop: 18px
   - Better spacing
   - More prominent

4. ✅ 2-Card Layout
   - Side-by-side on desktop
   - Equal visual weight
   - Clear separation
   - Balanced design

5. ✅ Proper Action Spacing
   - Primary → Secondary: 16px
   - Secondary → Remove: 32px
   - Border separator
   - No misclick risk

6. ✅ Remove Danger Zone
   - Border-top separator
   - Narrower container (max-w-sm)
   - Text button (not card)
   - Underline on hover only


SPACING SYSTEM
──────────────

Mobile:
- Global: space-y-3 (12px)
- Cards: gap-3 (12px)
- CTAs: space-y-3 (12px)
- Before remove: pt-4 + mt-2 + border = ~20px

Desktop:
- Global: space-y-4 (16px)
- Cards: gap-4 (16px) 
- CTAs: space-y-4 (16px)
- Before remove: pt-6 + mt-3 + border = ~32px


VISUAL HIERARCHY
────────────────

Level 1: HEADLINE
  ↓
Level 2: 2 CARDS (Problem)
  - Card 1: KPI proof
  - Card 2: Explanation
  ↓
Level 3: ACTIONS (Solution)
  - Primary: Activate (dominant)
  - Secondary: Keep free (clear)
  - Tertiary: Remove (danger zone)


PSYCHOLOGY
──────────

Flow:
1. Headline: "Remove?"
2. Cards: "I lost 7 clients"
3. Primary CTA: "I should activate"
4. Keep free: "Or just keep it"
5. Remove: "That's risky"

Conversion:
- Loss aversion (big number)
- Social proof (competitors)
- Friction reduction (<30 sec)
- Safety net (keep free)
- Danger zone (remove)
