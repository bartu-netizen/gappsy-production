## Copy Optimization Strategy

### Power Words Used
- "Start capturing" (ownership)
- "Clients lost to competitors" (direct)
- "Getting attention" (social proof)
- "Daily requests" (urgency)
- "Start receiving" (benefit)
- "Already getting interest" (reframe)

### Psychological Triggers
1. **Personalization** - Uses agency name and state
2. **Loss Aversion** - Quantified client loss
3. **Social Proof** - "Getting attention"
4. **Scarcity** - "Daily requests"
5. **Clarity** - Explains cause/effect
6. **Urgency** - "Happening daily"
7. **Reframe** - "Capturing" not "starting"
8. **Trust** - Transparent remove option

### Friction Reduction
- "30 seconds" (time commitment clear)
- "Start receiving" (immediate benefit)
- "Already getting interest" (no cold start)
- Clear alternatives (no pressure)
