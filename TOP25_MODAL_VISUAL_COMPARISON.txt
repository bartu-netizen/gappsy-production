================================================================================
CONVERSION IMPACT
================================================================================

BEFORE: User Journey
1. Modal opens → Sees header + subtext
2. Reads "Already listed? Find your agency" (text link, low prominence)
3. May or may not notice the link (easy to miss)
4. Must scroll to see form fields
5. If scrolls, finds form fields
6. May think "this is complex" due to scroll friction
7. Lower agency owner claim-through rate

AFTER: User Journey
1. Modal opens → Sees header + subtext + prominent button + first form fields
2. Immediately notices "Already listed? Find your agency →" (button, clear CTA)
3. Two clear options visible:
   a) Find existing listing (prominent button)
   b) Submit new agency (form fields visible below)
4. No scroll required to make decision
5. Lower friction = higher completion rate
6. Higher agency owner claim-through rate

FRICTION REDUCTION:
- Scroll elimination: -1 interaction (saves friction)
- Button prominence: Easier decision making
- Information visibility: Users see all options immediately
